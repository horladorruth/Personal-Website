About Me

More than just a teacher

Global Educator

Teaching across cultures in Taiwan and the Philippines, embracing diversity and bringing international perspectives to the classroom.

Inclusive Advocate

Specialized in Special Education with expertise in Filipino Sign Language and creating learning environments where every child thrives.

Creative Innovator

Implementing Reggio Emilia and play-based approaches that transform learning into joyful exploration and discovery.

I believe every child is a unique universe waiting to be explored. With over seven years of teaching experience spanning preschool to elementary education, I've had the privilege of guiding hundreds of young minds on their learning journeys.

My path has taken me from the vibrant streets of Iloilo, Philippines, to the bustling classrooms of Taipei, Taiwan. Each experience has enriched my understanding of how children learn, grow, and discover their place in the world.

Whether I'm facilitating a tinkering project, teaching phonics through song, or supporting a child with special needs, my goal remains the same: to create magical moments where learning feels like play and every child feels valued.

My Teaching Philosophy

The principles that guide my classroom

Every Child Blooms Differently

I celebrate individual learning styles and developmental timelines, providing differentiated instruction that meets each child where they are.

Play is the Work of Childhood

Through hands-on exploration, creative projects, and inquiry-based learning, children construct knowledge that lasts a lifetime.

Community Creates Belonging

Building strong partnerships with families, nurturing peer relationships, and fostering a classroom culture of respect and kindness.

Curiosity Drives Learning

I spark wonder and encourage questions, creating environments where children feel safe to explore, experiment, and even make mistakes.
